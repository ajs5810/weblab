The Life and Career of Drake

Originally born in Canada, Drake rose to fame as a rapper in the early 2000s for his ability to combine both pop and hip hop into his hits.

Early Life

Before Drake became a famous rapper, he started acting on a teen show which gave him his first break and the financial freedom to pursue music.

Music Career

Drake released his first big hit , which landed him #2 on the Billboard Hot 100 in 2009. Since then, he has released multiple top albums.

Major Albums

  1. Certified Lover Boy (2021)
  2. Take Care (2011)
  3. Views (2016)

Fun Facts

  • Founded his own record label, OVO Sound.
  • Holds the record for most Billboard Hot 100 entries.
  • First artist to hit 50 billion streams on Spotify.
